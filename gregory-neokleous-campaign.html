Who I Am

A Lifetime Connected to Kyrenia

Not just another candidate — a voice that carries the memory, the struggle, and the hope of our community.

Even if we are not there, we are not gone.

I am running for Kyrenia because our voice deserves to be heard — not symbolically, but actively. Every decision, every policy, every moment of representation matters for families who cannot physically return home.

This is about more than politics. It's about identity, memory, and the future we build for the next generation. I will represent every displaced family with the strength and determination our community deserves.

My commitment is simple: to fight tirelessly for our rights, to keep our story alive, and to ensure that Kyrenia's voice remains powerful and present.
